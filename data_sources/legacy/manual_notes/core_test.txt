Astral Core is used for SS gear progression and can be valuable when it helps reach an astral forge breakpoint.

Xeno Core is used for Xeno pet progression and is usually more valuable long-term when the player is close to a Xeno awakening breakpoint.

Resonance Chip is used for tech/resonance progression. It can be useful for immediate stat gains, but may be weaker if it does not unlock a major breakpoint.
