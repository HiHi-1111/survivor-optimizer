@ EFFECTS EXPLANATION

@ Adds 0.5x base damage per hit (NOT +50% damage bonus) when enemy is ot
100% health, scaling down to O when enemy is ot 50% health. Examples:
90% Enemy Health: +0.40x base damage per hit
75% Enemy Health: +0.25x base damage per hit
50% Enemy Health: no bonus

@ Adds 1.0x base damage per hit to enemies with full health. With the first effect,
the first hit is +1.5x base damage. This is NOT a +150% damage bonus.

© All passive effects are multiplied by 1.15x before being applied to your survivor.
For example, if you get a 5* Hi-Power Bullet, your damage bonus is 50% x 1.15 =
57.5%.

e Your first passive can be evolved, adding another level. For example, if you have
HP Bullet, each level adds 10%. If you evo it, it goes from +50% dmg to +60%
ding (excluding the 1.15x red effect). For Fitness Guide, each level adds 20%, so
evo'd is +120% HP. If you pick a character-exclusive passive first (such as
Survivor Instinct), then your 2nd passive will be the one that can be evolved.

Base damage is your attack with additional skill damage bonuses (such as from survivor or pets), and other damage bonuses included
(hi-power bullet, dominator state, etc.) It is the damage that a 1* Drill Shot would do.

EXAMPLE: Assume your base damage is 10k, you're using a 1* Lightchaser, and your enemy starts out with 100k health.
Lightchaser's 1* skill damage is 2x base damage (see #game-information for weapon/skill damage multipliers), so normally it will do 20k

damage.

~ 1st Hit: 35k damage. (Normal 20k damage + 1.5x base damage from Eternal Necklace purple effect)
~ 2nd Hit: 21.5k damage. (Enemy is now ot 65% health, so Eternal Necklace green effect only adds 0.15x base damage, or 1.5k

damage)

~ 3rd Hit: 20k damage. (Enemy is now below 50% health, so no damage bonus.)

WM Crits will scale the increased damage.

(} Purple effect is always active, so +1.5x base damage for every hit.

In theory, you get the most effect out of weapons/skills that do high-frequency damage (examples might be Eternal Boots fire
trail, Gatling gun). Gatling gun's damage multiplier is 2x, so adding an extra 1.5x is equivalent to a 75% damage bonus. Spirit
Shuriken's damage multiplier is 5.625 with +30% kunai damage from blue effect, so adding 1.5x is equivalent to a ~20.5% damage
bonus. Hard to tell whether Eternal Necklace’s damage bonus makes one weapon more effective than others, as Spirit Shuriken

fires pretty quickly already.
