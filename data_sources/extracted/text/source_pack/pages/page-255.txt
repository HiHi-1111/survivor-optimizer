Doomsteed + SS Boots 
Doomsteed wants movement and boss contact. 
SS Boots also reward movement and stacking. 
So Doomsteed and SS Boots should have a synergy flag. 
Fields: 
SynergyID = DOOMSTEED_SSBOOTS 
Condition = Player can keep moving around/touching boss 
Stats = MovementSpeed, GlacialBloodline, BossCollisionDamage 
DPSRelevant = High 
Hoverboard + shield systems 
Hoverboard uses Thorn Shield and Shield Resonance. 
This links to Shield Damage Boost and shield-based damage systems. 
Fields: 
SynergyID = HOVERBOARD_SHIELD_DAMAGE 
Stats = ShieldDamageBoost, SkillDamage, DamageToChilled 
DPSRelevant = High 
Scooter + Laceration / Weaken 
Scooter applies Laceration through discs. 
It also gives damage to Weakened targets and Crit Damage. 
Fields: 
SynergyID = SCOOTER_LACERATE_WEAKEN 
Stats = DamageToLacerated, DamageToWeakened, CritDamage 
DPSRelevant = Medium/High
