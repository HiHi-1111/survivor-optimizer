Pet skill registry 
Source: Pet_Skills_2024-09-09 and Pet_Awakening_Guide_2024-10-25 
This is the skill dictionary. These skills should be stored separately from pets because different 
pets unlock different skills at different rarity/star levels. 
 
Owner / Survivor buff skills 
These affect the player, not only the pet. 
Barrier 
Increases player's Range Damage Reduction by 10/15% 
DPSRelevant = No 
Diligence 
Increases player's Battle EXP gain by 5/7.5/10% 
DPSRelevant = Indirect only 
Encouragement 
Increases player's Skill Damage by 3/5% 
DPSRelevant = Yes 
Guard 
Increases player's Collision Damage Reduction by 10% 
DPSRelevant = No 
Inspiration 
Increases player's Crit Damage by 6/10% 
DPSRelevant = Yes 
Motivation 
Increases player's Crit Rate by 3/4/5% 
DPSRelevant = Yes 
Optimizer notes 
For your damage-only calculator, the big owner-buff pet skills are: 
Encouragement = Skill Damage 
Inspiration = Crit Damage 
Motivation = Crit Rate
