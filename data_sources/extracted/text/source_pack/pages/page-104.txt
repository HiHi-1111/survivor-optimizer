What they do 
Collectibles give: 
Global ATK 
Crit Rate 
Crit Damage 
Gear-specific bonuses 
SS gear set bonuses 
Skill damage bonuses 
Target-specific damage bonuses 
Unlock requirement 
The source set has collectible chest odds and costs, but the exact chapter unlock is not shown 
in the chapter unlock chart. So in the optimizer, mark unlock as: 
UnlockRequirement = Collectible system available / event or chest access 
Do not invent a chapter until confirmed. 
Optimizer rule 
Collectibles must connect to exact gear/skill systems. 
Example: 
Some collectibles boost SS Weapon Energy Overload. 
Some boost SS Belt/Armor/Gloves effects. 
Some give global Crit Rate or Crit Damage. 
Collectibles are later-game but very important for final DPS.
