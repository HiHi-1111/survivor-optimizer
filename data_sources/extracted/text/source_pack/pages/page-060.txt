Pet awakening investment advice from the 
source 
The source gives a clear priority rule: 
1. Take Rex or Croaky to R5 first. Both are very close in total damage. 
2. After that, take each pet to Y5 with a balanced approach to get the most ATK per crystal. 
  Example: all pets to Y2, then all to Y3, etc. 
3. Finally, take one pet from each gift type to R5. 
It also says: 
Since Y5 to R5 takes 280 crystals, the average for all 5 levels is about 36.5 crystals. 
It is not worth rushing R1-R3 at worse rates when cheaper Y-level sources still exist.
