H) Optimizer scoring rules 
1. Do not recommend resonance before Legendary tech. 
2. Do not recommend Twinborn before both paired techs are Legendary. 
3. Do not recommend Tech Overload before 3000 Twinborn resonance. 
4. Value ATK, skill damage, crit, vulnerability, poison, chill, laceration, weaken, and shield 
damage. 
5. Store HP but mark IgnoreForDPS. 
6. Normal Drone resonance is good before Twinborn. 
7. Normal RPG/Molotov resonance becomes lower value once Twinborn options exist. 
8. Resonance chips should be spent by DPS gain per chip, not evenly across every tech.
