What relic cores do 
Relic cores are used for SS gear forge upgrades. 
They affect: 
Eternal Forge levels 
Void Forge levels 
Chaos Forge unlock path

Key relic core rule 
Spend relic cores by DPS gain per relic core, not by “highest rarity” or “newest item.” 
Important notes 
SS Weapon and SS Belt are usually high-value relic core targets. 
SS Gloves depend heavily on crit/passive scaling. 
SS Necklace becomes better once crit rate and passive level systems are strong. 
SS Armor is weak at base and should not be early priority.
