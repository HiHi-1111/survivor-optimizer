Optimizer rule 
Debuffs should be treated as their own damage layer. 
Important reason: multiple systems say “damage to Chilled,” “damage to Weakened,” “damage 
to Lacerated,” or “Vulnerability.” Those only matter if the debuff exists and has uptime.
