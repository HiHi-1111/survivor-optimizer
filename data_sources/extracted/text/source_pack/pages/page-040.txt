Pet | Pet Skill | Pet Awakening | Assist Skill | Pet Toy | Pet Affection | Pet DPS | Owner DPS Buff 
| Pet Crit | Pet Cooldown | Pet Inherited ATK | Pet Utility 
For your calculator, pets matter in two different ways: 
1.​ Direct pet damage — Murica/Eagle whirlwind, Crab bullets, Robot lasers, etc. 
2.​ Owner DPS buffs — Crit Rate, Crit Damage, Skill Damage, cooldown, pet skill damage, 
inherited ATK. 
HP, damage reduction, revival time, and invincibility should be stored, but mostly marked as 
ignore for DPS unless later you make a survival-mode version. 
 
Main pet structure

Primary Attack 
Fixed pet skill pool 
Awakening/star progression 
Assist skill compatibility 
Disposition 
Toy/affection bonus
