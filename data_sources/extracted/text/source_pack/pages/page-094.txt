What it does 
Tech resonance lets a main tech part gain bonus effects from support tech parts. It adds: 
Resonance Energy 
ATK/HP from resonance 
Special resonance skills 
Multiplier upgrades 
Drone wingbits 
RPG/C4 explosion bonuses 
Molotov/SF fire and poison bonuses 
Resonance energy values 
From Tech_Part_Resonance_Cost: 
Yellow tech part = 50 
Yellow +1 = 100 
Yellow +2 = 150 
Yellow +3 = 200 
Red = 300 
Red +1 = 400 
Red +2 = 550 
Red +3 = 700 
Red +4 = 850 
Rainbow / resonance-tier part = 1000 
Multiplier upgrade cost 
1.0 → 1.2 = 1 resonance chip 
1.2 → 1.4 = 1 
1.4 → 1.6 = 2 
1.6 → 1.8 = 3
