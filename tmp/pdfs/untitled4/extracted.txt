Shared SP survivor structure 
Each SP survivor has: 
Yellow stars 
●​ 1-star: unlocks exclusive weapon / base survivor skill. 
●​ 2-star: Survivor License, usually Survivor ATK +400 and Survivor HP +2000. 
●​ 3-star: improves the survivor’s main weapon or attack mechanic. 
●​ 4-star: Advanced Survivor License, usually Survivor ATK +1000 and Survivor HP 
+5000. 
●​ 5-star: All Survivors: Crit Rate +8%. 
●​ 6-star: big personal DPS upgrade, usually main weapon damage + survivor ATK +20%. 
Red awakening stars 
●​ Adds active awakening skill improvements. 
●​ Adds passive awakening skills. 
●​ Some give Teamwork Passive Slots, which matter a lot because awakened passive 
skills can be shared/equipped. 
●​ Costs appear to scale with awakening cores, pizza/slices, and blue materials. 
For calculator logic, 5 yellow stars is extremely important because it gives global crit rate. 
The HP parts are mostly trash for your DPS optimizer.

Formula logic for pet DPS 
Keep pet DPS separate first: 
PetDPS = 
 PetBaseAttackDamage 
* PetAttackFrequency 
* PetATKMultiplier 
* PetSkillDamageMultiplier 
* PetCritMultiplier 
* PetCooldownMultiplier 
* HitConsistencyMultiplier

Then owner DPS buffs from pets: 
OwnerDPSFromPetBuffs = 
 OwnerCritRateFromPet 
+ OwnerCritDamageFromPet 
+ OwnerSkillDamageFromPet 
+ PlayerATKFromPetAffection 
Do not mix these too early. A pet can be good because it does direct damage, or because it 
buffs the owner.
