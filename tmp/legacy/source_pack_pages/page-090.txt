SS gear is the late-game gear layer. Each slot has special systems: 
Slot 
Main function 
SS 
Weapon 
Starforged Havoc, Void Power, Energy Overload, Void Fissure, Chaos Meteor, 
boss damage loops. 
SS 
Necklace 
Extra skill choices, skill damage, energy shield effects. 
SS Gloves 
Weaken, damage to Weakened targets, Red Star passive scaling, crit damage 
scaling. 
SS Armor 
Energy levels, energy orbs, damage multiplier, skill damage, crit damage by 
energy level. 
SS Belt 
Crit rate ramping, Mega Crit breakpoints, Eternal Laser stored damage. 
SS Boots 
Movement speed, Chill, Glacial Bloodline stacks, damage to Chilled enemies, 
Vulnerability from ice arrows.

SS gear/fodder requirements 
Forge path level: E1-E5, V1-V5, Chaos levels where available 
Most important DPS breakpoints 
From SS Belt: 
Crit Rate >100% = Crit Damage +30% 
Crit Rate >130% = Skill Damage +30% 
Crit Rate >150% = Crit Damage +100% 
This means the optimizer must value Crit Rate extra hard when it crosses one of those 
breakpoints.
