6. SS Boots 
Main association 
SS Boots | Movement Speed | Chilled enemies | Crystal Trail | Ice Blocks | Glacial Bloodline | 
Vulnerability | Damage stacks 
The boots are very conditional. They are strong if the build can maintain chill/glacial stacks. 
Base / rarity effects 
Base Movement Speed +3 
HP +15% 
Crystal Trail: 
Moving leaves a trail of ice blocks lasting 10 seconds. 
Ignites key explosions when they expire. 
Enemies touching blocks or hit by explosions are Chilled for 10 seconds. 
Unstackable. 
HP +25% 
Damage to Chilled enemies +15% 
Glacial Bloodline: 
Move to start charging. 
The faster the speed, the faster the charge. 
Charge weakens when movement stops. 
At full charge, gain 1 Glacial Bloodline stack. 
Each stack gives +12% damage, lasting 15 seconds. 
Max 5 stacks.
