5. Teamwork Passive Slots 
Most SP survivors unlock Teamwork Passive Slots at awakening levels: 
●​ A4: Unlocks 1 Teamwork Passive Slot 
●​ A6: Teamwork Passive Slot +1 
This matters because survivor awakening is not just “upgrade survivor.” It changes how many 
passive skills can be equipped and shared. 
Suggested fields: 
TeamworkPassiveSlotUnlock_A4 = 1​
 TeamworkPassiveSlotUnlock_A6 = +1
