14. Random component stats display 
The in-game “Random Component Stats” section shows stats from the components installed in 
the mount you are using. 
For optimizer, do not rely only on displayed stats. 
Use two separate totals: 
DisplayedMainMountStats = 
   Sum(components installed in equipped mount) 
 
SyncedInactiveMountStats = 
   Sum(components installed in inactive mounts * that mount's sync rate) 
 
TotalMountComponentStats = 
   DisplayedMainMountStats + SyncedInactiveMountStats 
This matters because inactive mount stats may not show the same way, but they still matter 
through sync.
