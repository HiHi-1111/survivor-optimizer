Misc pet skills 
Nine Lives 
When player and pet take fatal damage, nullifies that attack and heals allies for 50% HP. 
Cooldown: 100 seconds. 
DPSRelevant = No 
Satellite Cannon 
Guides satellite to blast a shot at a certain area. 
Damage: 1000% ATK. 
Cooldown: 5 seconds. 
+250% damage when owner levels up. 
DPSRelevant = Yes 
Time Stop 
When player's HP is below 30%, stops time and freezes enemies for 5 seconds. 
Player and pet are invincible during it. 
Cooldown: 60 seconds. 
DPSRelevant = No, but useful utility 
 
Assist skills 
Assist skills are separate from the deployed pet’s normal skill set. These should be modeled as: 
AssistPetSkill | AssistPet | DeployedPetBuff | InBattleScaling 
The assist skills shown: 
Boost 
For every 5 owner levels, pet Range increases by 5/6/7.5/10%. 
DPSRelevant = Medium/High 
Durable 
For every 5 owner levels, pet shot Duration increases by 8/10/12/15%. 
DPSRelevant = Medium/High if pet projectile duration matters 
Penetration 
For every 10/8/6/5 owner levels, penetrated units of pet shot +1.
