Optimizer scoring rule 
For SS gear recommendations, don’t rank upgrades only by rarity. Rank by: 
1. Does it increase direct DPS? 
2. Does it unlock a crit breakpoint? 
3. Does it add a debuff damage multiplier? 
4. Does it improve boss damage specifically? 
5. Does it scale with stats the player already has? 
6. What is the relic core cost? 
7. Is the condition realistic for the player’s mode? 
For your current damage-only style, the calculator should heavily favor: 
Crit Rate breakpoint upgrades 
Void Fissure / Vulnerability upgrades 
Energy Overload stack upgrades
