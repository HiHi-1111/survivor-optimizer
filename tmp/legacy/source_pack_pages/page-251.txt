SpreadValue = Sum(ComponentValue * MountSyncRateOr1)

Each mount has its own line effects. A line effect activates when a horizontal row is complete. 
Completed row = no black/empty cell in that row. 
Completed rows are highlighted gold. 
Only line effects from the equipped mount count.
