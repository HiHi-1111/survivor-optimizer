Important conclusion for the optimizer 
The pet system is not “just pick Murica forever.” 
The source data says: 
Murica = strong direct pet DPS, especially through whirlwind, cooldown, Crush, and assist 
scaling. 
Croaky = strong long-term owner/pet DPS mix. 
Rex = strong long-term owner crit support and total damage contender. 
Crab = real pet DPS scaling through 500% attack, crit rate, cooldown, and inherited ATK. 
Robot = high theoretical laser/satellite damage but hit consistency matters. 
Turtle/Fish = more utility/survival than damage.
