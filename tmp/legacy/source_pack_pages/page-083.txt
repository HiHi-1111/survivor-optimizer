1. Crit Rate breakpoint system 
The SS Belt creates major breakpoints: 
Crit Rate >100% = Crit Damage +30% 
Crit Rate >130% = Skill Damage +30% 
Crit Rate >150% = Crit Damage +100% 
This means Crit Rate is not linear. If an upgrade pushes the player over 100/130/150%, it 
becomes more valuable. 
 
2. Debuff families involved 
This SS gear source connects to multiple debuffs: 
Weaken 
Chill 
Vulnerability 
Void Fissure damage taken 
That means this source must connect to your Boss_Debuffs table later. 
 
3. Energy Shield / Energy Level are separate systems 
Do not mix these up. 
Energy Shield = shield HP system 
Energy Level = armor/orb damage multiplier system
